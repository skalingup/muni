Installing Muni
===============

Muni is a self-contained Mac reading app — nothing else to install.
Works on any Mac (Apple Silicon and Intel), macOS 11 Big Sur or newer.

1. Open Muni.dmg.
2. Drag the Muni icon onto the Applications folder.
3. Open Muni:
   - Because Muni isn't signed with an Apple Developer certificate, macOS will
     refuse the first normal double-click ("Apple cannot check it for malware").
   - RELIABLE WAY: open Terminal and run this one line, then double-click Muni:
         xattr -dr com.apple.quarantine /Applications/Muni.app
   - OR: try to open it, then go to  System Settings ▸ Privacy & Security,
     scroll down, and click "Open Anyway".

That's it. Open a PDF (File ▸ Open PDF, or the Library) and read.
